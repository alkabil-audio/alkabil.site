?

Alkabil started as a way for a small group of people to release and put music out there. Moving things non-linearly.

SOME ANSWERS

Do you accept demos?

We sorta do. Please send demos to info@alkabil.audio. We don't really have time to reply to everything (or much of anything), but we do give an honest listen.

ARE THERE SHOWS?

We currently do more bespoke events with partner Sílbaca. We are planning on ways to get our artists out there when we can.

HOW DO ARTISTS GET PAID?

Artists retain 100% creative rights. We really only help with some promotion, but most importantly getting music out there in all services.

WHAT ABOUT AI?

Everything we release is made by people. We don't sign AI or synthetic artists, and we don't license our music, voices, likenesses, or metadata to AI training or datasets — that's written into every artist agreement, not just a stance we say out loud.

Ordinary studio tools are still tools; the line is anything that generates the substance of the work in place of a human. We can't police every platform that scrapes the web, but we're careful about who we work with, and we chase down what we find.
